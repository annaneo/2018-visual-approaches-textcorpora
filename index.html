Inscriptions in their Spatial Context

BMBF Project (2012-2015)

http://spatialhumanities.de/

  • Goal: provide new methods for the analysis of spatial data by connecting spatial data (terrestrical laser scans) with Verbindung von Raumdaten (terrestric laserscanning) with research data (RDF)
  • Webbased tool to capture and semantically annotate spatial geometries in the point cloud (Generic Viewer)
  • Webservice to harvest and extract semantic statements from XML-based reserach data(XTriples)
